# Supplier Insurance Requirements Policy

## Purpose
Define minimum insurance coverage required from suppliers performing work under Buyer purchase orders.

## Requirements
1. General Liability: Minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence.
2. Workers' Compensation: Statutory minimum.
3. Professional Liability: Required for engineering/design services.
4. Certificates: Provide certificates of insurance upon request and upon renewal.

## Exceptions
Any deviation from required coverage must be approved by Risk Management.
