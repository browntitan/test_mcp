# Standard Payment Terms Policy

## Purpose
Define acceptable payment terms for supplier contracts and purchase orders.

## Requirements
1. Default: Net 30 is standard unless an exception is approved.
2. Extended Terms: Net 45 or Net 60 require finance approval and documented justification.
3. Discounts: Early payment discounts must be evaluated against cost of capital.
4. Disputes: Payment may be withheld only for documented disputed amounts.

## Records
Retain approval artifacts for non-standard terms.
