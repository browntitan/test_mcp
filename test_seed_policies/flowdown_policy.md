# Mandatory Flowdown Clauses Policy

## Purpose
Ensure suppliers and subcontractors are bound to required FAR/DFARS and Buyer policies.

## Requirements
1. Applicability: Suppliers must identify which FAR/DFARS clauses apply based on contract type, value, and work scope.
2. Flowdown: Applicable clauses must be flowed down to subcontractors at every tier.
3. Evidence: Supplier must maintain flowdown matrices and subcontract language evidencing compliance.

## Common Flowdowns
- DFARS 252.204-7012 (Cyber)
- ITAR/EAR controls
- Quality requirements
